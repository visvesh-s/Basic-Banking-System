      OREO BANK, is a high quality, customer centric and service driven Bank. Since inception in 2004, OREO BANK has grown into a ‘Full Service Commercial Bank’ providing a complete range of products, services and technology driven digital offerings, catering to corporate, MSME & retail customers.

      OREO BANK operates its Investment banking, Merchant banking & Brokerage businesses through OREO SECURITIES and its Mutual Fund business through OREO Asset Management (India) Limited, both wholly owned subsidiaries of the Bank. Headquartered in Mumbai, it has a pan-India presence across all 28 states and 9 Union Territories in India including an IBU at GIFT City, and a Representative Office in Abu Dhabi.
